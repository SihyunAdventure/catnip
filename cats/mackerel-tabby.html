Coat & markings 코트와 무늬

The mackerel tabby takes its name from the fish it resembles: fine, narrow stripes run vertically down a silver-grey body like the bones of a mackerel, branching off a darker line along the spine. It is the most common tabby pattern of all, and in Korea the grey mackerel cat is so familiar on the streets that "godeungeo" — the word for the fish — is simply what people call it. Every mackerel wears the tabby badge on the forehead, a clean "M" above watchful eyes, with the coat cooling from pale pewter to charcoal along the stripes. White often appears on the chin, chest, and paws. In catnip the mackerel cat is rendered as thousands of individual grey strands, so the fine striping isn't a texture painted on — it emerges from the fur itself, parting and settling as you stroke it.

고등어 태비는 이름 그대로 생선을 닮았어요 — 은회색 몸통을 따라 좁고 촘촘한 줄무늬가 세로로 흐르는 모습이 마치 고등어의 가시 같고, 등줄기의 짙은 선에서 갈라져 나오죠. 모든 태비 무늬 중 가장 흔하며, 한국 길거리에서 워낙 익숙해 아예 생선 이름 그대로 "고등어"라 불립니다. 이마엔 태비 특유의 또렷한 "M" 자가 있고, 줄무늬를 따라 털빛이 연한 백랍색에서 짙은 차콜까지 오가요. 턱·가슴·발엔 흰색이 자주 섞입니다. catnip 속 고등어는 수천 가닥의 회색 털로 그려지기 때문에, 촘촘한 줄무늬는 위에 덧칠한 무늬가 아니라 털 자체에서 배어 나와요 — 쓰다듬으면 줄이 갈라졌다 다시 가라앉습니다.

Personality 성격 아키타입

If the cheese cat is all warmth, the mackerel cat is all composure. catnip draws it as the cool observer: it sizes up a room before it commits to it, watches a new hand for a moment before it leans in, and keeps its circle small. But the ones inside that circle are kept for life. A mackerel cat's affection is earned rather than given away, which is exactly why it feels like a reward — the slow blink from across the room, the quiet decision to settle on your lap on its own terms. For someone who wants a calm, dignified companion with a loyal streak underneath, the mackerel tabby is the steady one.
