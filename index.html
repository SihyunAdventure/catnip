about catnip

catnip is a tiny self-care companion, with a cat. Adopt one, then check in on yourself each day — drink a glass of water, take a short walk, breathe. Tend to the small things and your cat grows: it's drawn entirely in code, about 25,000 fur strands in a single WebGL draw call, with spring-field physics that parts and ripples the fur under your cursor. Pet it until it purrs, feed it, and slip into the quiet room to focus while it naps. When you need a breather there are eight little games to rest with: chase the laser pointer to earn jelly 🐾, crown a winner in the cutie world cup, meet your match in the which-cat-are-you quiz, settle feline dilemmas in cat balance, dangle a fishing rod and let them grab it, tap along to a kneading rhythm, time the jump in the box, or brush out the tangles in a grooming session. Browse every breed you've met so far in the cat book. No install, no login, free.
